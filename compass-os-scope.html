02 · The off-rampYou can step off at any milestone boundary.

From the Statement of Work
"Compass can pull out at any milestone boundary with full ownership of everything delivered to that point. No further fees. No clawback."

Each milestone earns the next. Sign-off at every boundary. If a milestone isn't accepted, the next invoice doesn't issue and the work continues until it is. The strongest commercial off-ramp is at M2 sign-off, $39,000 in: you've got the audit foundation plus the Sales Engine in the team's hands. M3 + M4 only commit Compass to the final $11,000 once you've seen M2 land.
