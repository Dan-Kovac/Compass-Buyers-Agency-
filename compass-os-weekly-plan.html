Compass OS · Delivery Plan

Week-by-week breakdown of the Stage 1 Statement of Work.

Compass Buyers Agency · 4 months · $50,000 + GST · Three streams · Four monthly milestones · Prepared by Dan for Nick and Chris

You are reading the Delivery Plan. 16 weeks, ticket by ticket.
Two companion documents: Proposal for why this works, Statement of Work for what we commit to.
What's in this plan.
  • 16 weeks, ticket by ticket. Grouped by stream: Web app (A), Bruce on WhatsApp (B), Agent space (C).
  • What Compass does each week. Most weeks: a 30-minute Monday catch-up and a 5-minute Friday Loom.
  • Three milestone gates: M1, M2, M3. Each one carries an acceptance test, taken straight from the SOW.
  • Week 0: account and environment setup. Every account, every brand asset, every decision Compass locks in before week 1.

Who builds this

Same team the SOW describes. Three of us on the build, plus a small specialised agent set behind us for drafting and scaffolding. Agents draft, we review, then it ships.

Roles you'll see on tickets

Delivery Lead (Dan)
Plans the work, briefs the team, runs the Monday catch-ups, owns every sign-off.
Engineer
Writes the code. Builds the database, the web app, Bruce, the agent space. Every change reviewed before it ships.
Frontend Designer
Layout, type, spacing, motion. Makes every screen look like Compass.
Copywriter
Every word in the app and in Bruce's mouth. Tuned to Compass's voice. Never ships raw.
DevOps
Hosting, deployment, security, monitoring, the WhatsApp plumbing. The bits that keep everything running.
Tester
Tries to break the build before clients do. Walks the end-to-end flow before each milestone.
