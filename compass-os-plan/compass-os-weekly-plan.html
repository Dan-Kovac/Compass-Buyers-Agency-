Compass OS

16-week build plan, week by week.

Compass Buyers Agency · 4 months · $50,000 + GST · Three streams: Web app (A), Bruce on WhatsApp (B), Agent space (C)

What you'll see in this document.
  • What gets built each week, broken down by stream.
  • What we need from Compass each week (mostly a 30-minute Monday catch-up plus a few demos and sign-offs).
  • Three milestone gates (M1, M2, M3) where you formally accept what's been delivered before we move on.
How to read the tables. Every row is one ticket. Tickets carry a stream (A / B / C / Compass), a role, an effort estimate (S / M / L), what they depend on, and concrete acceptance criteria. The same tickets are imported into Notion so we can track them as we go.
Stage 1 · Weeks 1-4

Notes for Compass

  • What you do each week: the "Compass" rows are the only items needing your time. Most weeks that's a 30-min Monday catch-up plus reviewing one demo or sign-off.
  • What we do each week: everything in streams A, B and C. You don't need to manage these. You need to see they're moving, which is what the Monday catch-up is for.
  • Why this works: three parallel streams means progress is always visible somewhere. If one stream hits a snag, the other two keep moving and we re-balance.
  • Milestones are gates, not deadlines. M1, M2 and M3 are the points where you formally accept what's been built. If something isn't right, we don't move on.
