Compass OS

16-week build plan, week by week.

Compass Buyers Agency · 4 months · $50,000 + GST · Three streams: Web app (A), Bruce on WhatsApp (B), Agent space (C)

What's in this document.
  • What gets built each week, grouped by the three streams.
  • Exactly what Nick needs to do each week, in plain English. Most weeks: one 30-minute catch-up.
  • Who is doing the work, and what each role actually means.
  • Three milestone gates (M1, M2, M3) where Compass formally accepts what's been delivered.

Who's building this

A small, focused team:

  • Daniel as Delivery Lead. Single point of contact for Compass. Plans the work, runs the Monday catch-ups, owns every sign-off, and is accountable for what ships.
  • A local engineer working alongside Daniel on the build. Engineering depth and review on every code change before it reaches production.
  • A purpose-built agent stack running vetted, tested skills refined across multiple prior projects. The agents handle drafting, scaffolding, and the long tail of repetitive build work.

The agents aren't the headline. They're the tools that let a small team deliver studio-scale throughput. What makes the work above standard is the craft layered on top:

  • Quality of inputs. Brand tokens, voice samples, data models, and constraints are curated before the agents touch them. Agents working from the right source produce work that's already most of the way there.
  • Meticulous refinement of outputs. Every artefact is reviewed against quality bars from past projects and refined across multiple passes. Nothing ships raw.
  • Human-reviewed integration. Code is reviewed by the engineer. Copy and design are reviewed by Daniel. Nothing reaches production without human eyes on it.

Compass gets the speed and consistency of an automated build with the judgement of a senior team. One point of contact, one quality bar, faster than a traditional agency.

The roles you'll see in this plan

Delivery Lead — Daniel
Daniel's role personally. Plans the work, runs catch-ups, briefs the team, runs demos, owns every sign-off. Compass's single point of contact.
Engineer
Code, database, integrations, the full stack. Daniel and the local engineer working with engineering agents. Every change reviewed before merge.
Frontend Designer
Layout, type, spacing, colour, motion. Design agents working from Compass's brand assets, with Daniel directing and reviewing every screen.
Copywriter
UI copy, emails, Bruce's voice. Copy agents working from Compass's voice samples and tone-of-voice guide. Refined against brief, never shipped raw.
DevOps
Hosting, deployment, security, backups, monitoring. Infrastructure agents under the engineer's review.
Tester
Cross-device QA, end-to-end flows, milestone gates. QA agents validating against acceptance criteria. Daniel reviews flagged issues before sign-off.
