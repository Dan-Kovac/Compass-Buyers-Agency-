Compass OS

16-week build plan, week by week.

Compass Buyers Agency · 4 months · $50,000 + GST · Three streams: Web app (A), Bruce on WhatsApp (B), Agent space (C)

What's in this document.
  • What gets built each week, grouped by the three streams.
  • Exactly what Nick needs to do each week, in plain English. Most weeks: one 30-minute catch-up.
  • Who is doing the work, and what each role actually means.
  • Three milestone gates (M1, M2, M3) where Compass formally accepts what's been delivered.

Who's building this

The work is delivered by Daniel as the lead, orchestrating a team of specialist AI agents. Each agent is purpose-built for its discipline: it carries the right skills, the right tools, and the right context for that slice of the work. Daniel briefs them, reviews their output, and is accountable for everything that ships. You deal with one person. You get the throughput of a small studio.

That means progress is fast, traceable, and consistent. Every ticket in this plan has a named role attached so you always know what kind of skill is being applied.

The roles you'll see in this plan

Engineer
Writes the code. Builds the database, the web app, the WhatsApp bot, and the agent dashboard. The bulk of the build.
Frontend Designer
Decides how it looks and feels. Layout, type, spacing, colour, motion. Makes sure every screen is on-brand and pleasant to use.
Copywriter
Writes the words. Button labels, error messages, emails, and most importantly Bruce's voice on WhatsApp. Australian English throughout.
DevOps
Sets up infrastructure, hosting, security, backups, and the production launch. The plumbing that makes everything reliable.
Tester
Tries to break it before real users do. Runs cross-device checks, files bugs, runs the end-to-end flow before each milestone.
Delivery Lead
Plans the work, runs the Monday catch-ups, keeps the Notion board in shape, runs demos, and handles UAT sign-offs. Nick's main point of contact alongside Daniel.

The bottom line for Nick

  • About 20 hours of your time over four months. Mostly Monday catch-ups, three milestone sign-offs, one training session, and a handful of content drops (FAQs, voice samples).
  • Three formal gates. M1, M2, M3. If something isn't right, we don't move on.
  • One person to deal with. Daniel orchestrates a team of specialist AI agents. You don't need to manage developers, designers, or copywriters — Daniel does that.
  • Everything is tracked in Notion. Every ticket in this document lives on the Compass OS board. You can see exactly what's done, what's in progress, and what's coming.
